About Kindtech

Our Mission

As data scientists and machine learning engineers working in the private sector, we've witnessed firsthand the unique challenges faced by the charity sector. The problems they encounter are fundamentally different, and there simply aren't enough resources dedicated to developing technology solutions specifically for their needs.

The tech divide in data and AI usage is widening rapidly, leaving many charitable organizations behind. We're committed to closing this gap by building open source technology projects that address the specific needs of the charity sector.

Through Kindtech, we aim to bridge this technological gap by creating accessible, ethical, and purpose-driven solutions that empower charities to leverage the power of data and AI for greater social impact.
